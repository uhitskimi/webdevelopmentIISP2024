Oliver Lester

This here is Oliver Lester. He was born December 24th of 2019, making him four years old! While Oliver does not like to be picked up, he will never refuse affection and loves to be pet and rubbed. Oliver had once attempted to run away, but after experiencing that freewill, he has not attempted to since. Some of his favorite snacks are apples and carrots!
